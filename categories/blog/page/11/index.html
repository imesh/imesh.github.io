IMAP To Your Rescue

How do you manage your mail box? May be you are using a mail client at your home computer, a web base mail client at your office and also may be another mail client in your mobile. If so you may have experienced that the mails you have already read in your home computer are still in un-read state when you log in to your web base mail client. This is because the protocol that your home computer mail client is using does not update your mail status in the mail server. »

What is an OpenID?

An OpenID is a user identity (commonly known as a user id) which can be used to sign into different web sites without registering at each place where you required to login. I think this is an innovative concept but also there were very similar concepts being used before in web applications such as MSN ID. You can get your own OpenID for FREE by an OpenID service provider (which is an OpenID server), http://www.openid.org/, http://www.myopenid.com/. »
