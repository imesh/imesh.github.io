Richard Stallman In Sri Lanka

Richard M. Stallman the American software freedom activist who started the free software movement, the launcher of the GNU Project and the founder of the Free Software Foundation (FSF) is going to speak at the Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya at 1.30 PM on 16th January 2008 (Tomorrow) and at the Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT) Auditorium on 18th January 2008 at 10.00 AM.

This is going to be one of the greatest events in the year 2008. Don’t miss out!
